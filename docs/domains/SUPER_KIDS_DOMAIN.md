# SuperKids — Family & parenting Life Domain

Family and parenting platform spanning pregnancy through teenagers and parents.

## Feature surface
- Pregnancy tracking · Baby development · Child growth  
- Vaccination schedules · Medical appointments · School activities  
- Family calendar · Parenting AI · Nutrition · Sleep  
- Education milestones · Family tasks · Allowances  
- Shared memories · Emergency information · Shopping lists  
- Child-safety UX (school arrival, caregiver map) — **presence truth in SuperFind**

## Ecosystem
| Concern | Shared service / peer domain |
|---------|------------------------------|
| Vaccines / appointments | SuperHealth + After Calendar |
| Allowances | SuperFinance + After Wallet ledger |
| Family members | After Family graph |
| Shopping | SuperHome |
| School runs / car seats | SuperGarage |
| Holidays | SuperTravel |
| Emergency profile | After Cloud documents + Notifications |
| Location / Safe Zones / SOS | **SuperFind** (do not ship a private location stack) |

## Note on SuperFamily
Historical `SuperFamily` naming is superseded by **SuperKids** as the
consumer Life Domain for family & parenting. Shared family graph remains
an ecosystem service (`AfterFamilyGraph`), not a separate competing app.
