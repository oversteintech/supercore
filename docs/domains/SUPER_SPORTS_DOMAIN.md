# SuperSports — Sport & performance Life Domain

**Not a fitness-only app.** SuperSports is the Life Domain for personal
performance **and** live sports intelligence.

## Pillars

### A. Fitness OS
- Workout tracking · Running · Cycling  
- Nutrition · Health metrics · Sports communities  

### B. Live sports intelligence
- Live scores · Fixtures · League tables  
- Player statistics · Transfers  
- Favorite teams · Match notifications  
- Fantasy features  

### C. AI (extends After AI — never a private stack)
- AI match analysis · AI match summaries · AI sports assistant  

## Product feeling
Apple Fitness + Strava + SofaScore + modern sports intelligence — one module,
one After ID, one AI context shared with Health / Kids / Finance / News.

## Ecosystem events (examples)
- `sports.workout.completed` → Health, Calendar, Finance  
- `sports.match.kickoff` → Notifications  
- `sports.fantasy.lineup_locked` → Games (cross-interest)  
- `sports.injury.flagged` → Health  

## Ownership boundary
SuperSports owns sport & performance UX. Medical diagnosis stays in
SuperHealth; family school sports day logistics can surface in SuperKids via
events/calendar — not duplicate stacks.
