# SuperGames — Gaming Life Domain

## Owns
- Game library · Achievements  
- Steam integration · Epic integration · Console tracking  
- Wishlist · Gaming news · Gaming communities  
- AI recommendations (After AI skills)  

## Ecosystem
- Wishlist purchase intent → SuperFinance  
- Session length / wellbeing hints → SuperHealth (optional, user-gated)  
- Gaming news briefs → SuperNews  
- Family screen-time policies → SuperKids  

## Ports
Store adapters (Steam/Epic/console) are **product ports** behind
`after_core` networking — never hard-wire SDKs into the OS kernel.
